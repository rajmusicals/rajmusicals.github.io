Our Vision

At Raj Musicals, we don't believe in making profit, we believe in creating brilliant minds. Our diverse courses allow students from all walks of life to tailor our courses to their own pace and learning at their own convenience. We believe that learning music requires only immense passion and nothing else. With this motto we hope to be a small part of many musical journeys.

As an organization, Raj Musicals considers the following values above all others:

  • Delivering world-class education in music
  • Ensuring a motivating environment for all students
  • Prioritizing the growth of all our students above all other motives

Our Philosophy

Music helps the mind develop and grow, I have found this to be very true. It is a proven fact that participating in a music program will help develop your brain to a higher level and faster. Music actually improves communication between the right and left sides of the brain, allowing you to gain better comprehension and memorization skills. Well, in my opinion music is everything.

— Raj Kr. Choudhary
Founder

Services

Keyboard

Whether you dream of playing professionally, or you’d just like to serenade your friends and family at home, learning to play the keyboard can be a rewarding endeavor.

Raj Musicals helps you learn the keyboard for any purpose. With enough practice, you can learn to play your favorite songs and chords on the keyboard as a part of the basic course after which you graduate into the advance course.

Drums

Drums are often thought to be very difficult to learn. We at Raj Musicals don't believe that is the case. Our course has been specially tailored to make learning drumming a joyful experience.

Guitar

The guitar classes are aimed at creating a versatile performer. The objective of these courses is to learn different genres of music like Rock, Blues, Funk, etc. An understanding of these styles not only helps students in pursuing independent music but also commercial Bollywood and non Bollywood music.

The course has been devised so as to make the student comfortable with the guitar in the first 6 months itself, with confidence the utmost priority.

Music Production

Our goal is to help you understand the process of making music from beginning to end, so you can create tracks that meet the standards of “good music”, regardless of style. What’s important is that you understand the process and tools at your disposal, so it will be easier to construct a quality track that delivers the message intended.

We simplify music production by separating it into six basic stages:

  • Songwriting
  • Arranging
  • Tracking
  • Editing
  • Mixing
  • Mastering
